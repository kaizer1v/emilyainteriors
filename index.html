About The Designer

Meet Emily

With over a decade of experience in luxury interior design, Emily brings a refined eye and personalized approach to every project. Her philosophy centers on creating spaces that are both beautiful and deeply livable.

After studying at the Parsons School of Design and apprenticing under renowned designers in Paris and Milan, Emily founded Emily Interiors with a mission to transform houses into homes that tell their owners' unique stories.

Whether working on a complete furnishing project or refreshing a space through styling, Emily believes in the power of thoughtful details—perfect lighting, curated textures, and meaningful objects that spark joy.
